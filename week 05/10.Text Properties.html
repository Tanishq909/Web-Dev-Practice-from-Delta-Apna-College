Iron Man

Google me Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was co-created by writer and editor Stan Lee,developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. The character made his first appearance in Tales of Susoense and received his own title in Iron Man. In 1963, the charater founded the Avengers superhero team with Thor, Ant-Man,Wasp and the Hulk.
